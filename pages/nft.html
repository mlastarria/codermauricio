About The NFT Project

This is a 1/1 art collection of 100 hand drawn digital drawings that I'm doing for 100 consecutive days. Each of the drawings can be a lesson or new learning I'm sharing such as Kindness, Work ethic, friendship, calm and help others. Throughout the entire collection you will find positive and some negative everyday situations that reflect the young and cheerful spirit of our character that is compared to life itself.
